What is Orange Jam?

Orange Jam is an in-person game development hackathon for all ages and experience levels. Use any platform — from Scratch and game engines like Unity or Unreal to simple command line games. Everything is judged equally, so that anyone can jump in, create, and compete.

The Challenge

Given a theme, you have 10 hours to develop a video game or interactive experience.

AI-Friendly

AI tools are welcome to help level the playing field for everyone.

One-Day Event

Everything happens in one exciting day — with lunch, snacks, activities, and more.

Win Prizes

Cash, trophies, and sponsor-backed prizes await the top teams in each division.
